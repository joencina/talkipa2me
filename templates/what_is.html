What is IPA?

Having 44 phonemes, English has largely outgrown the 24-letter roman alphabet. There is simply no way to have anything close to a one-to-one relationship from phonemes to letters. On top of that, English inherited words from a widely diverse set of languages and decided to respect their etymology, which is why the spelling system is so incredibly inconsistent. The rest of this article will be exploring those inconsistencies while presenting how the use of the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA) can help to overcome the problem of not being able to read an English word without hearing it before.
